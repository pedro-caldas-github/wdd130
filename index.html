Recife, Pernambuco

Recife, the "Venice of Brazil," is a vibrant coastal city renowned for its many rivers and bridges. Its historic center, Recife Antigo, showcases beautiful colonial architecture and is a hub for culture and art. The city also boasts stunning beaches like Boa Viagem and a lively Carnival celebration featuring unique local music.
